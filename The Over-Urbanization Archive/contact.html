Purpose

The Over-Urbanization Archive was created to educate people about the impacts of rapid urban growth through modern research and global case studies.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions regarding the archive and its research.

Why was this archive created?

The archive was created to provide accessible research and case studies regarding the impacts of over-urbanization around the world.

What topics are covered?

The website focuses on social, economic, political, and environmental impacts of rapid urban growth.

Can students use this archive for research?

Yes. The website was specifically designed to support students, researchers, and anyone interested in urbanization studies.
